## vscode

下载 [vscode insiders 体验版](https://code.visualstudio.com/insiders/)

    安装remote develop插件

    如果Remote Host有代理:

    ~/.wgetrc

        use_proxy=on
        http_proxy=http://myproxy.proxy:xxxx
        https_proxy=http://myproxy.proxy:xxxx

    避免多次输入密码:

    ~/.ssh/config

    Host *
        ControlMaster auto
        ControlPath  ~/.ssh/%r@%h-%p
        ControlPersist  600
